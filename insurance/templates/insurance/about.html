About Our ML Model

Model Overview
Linear Regression Model

Our insurance cost prediction model uses a Linear Regression algorithm trained on historical healthcare insurance data. The model analyzes multiple factors to estimate annual insurance costs.

Key Features:
  • Age: Older individuals typically have higher insurance costs
  • BMI (Body Mass Index): Higher BMI correlates with increased health risks
  • Smoking Status: Smokers face significantly higher insurance costs
  • Number of Children: Family size affects insurance premiums
  • Geographic Region: Healthcare costs vary by location
  • Sex: Biological sex can influence insurance pricing
